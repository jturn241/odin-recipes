Pulled Pork

Description

Delicious pulled pork anyone can make in their back yard

This cook can take anywhere from 7 - 14 hours depending upon the size of your pork shoulder

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder, pork butt or Boston butt depending upon where you're from
  • Kosher salt
  • Your favorite dry rub
  • Time, and lots of it!
  • A few chunks of your prefered hardwood for added flavor

Recipe

  1. Remove shoulder from packaging and trim any loose fat
  2. Optional - cut shoulder in half to reduce cook time
  3. Dry brine shoulder by liberally coating all sides of the shoulder
  4. Store shoulder in fridge for a few hours, preferably overnight
  5. Prep grill by getting it up to a temp of 225-250 *F
  6. Once you've got a clean burn, ie. no thick white smoke - nice clear blue smoke - add your meat
  7. Cook until internal temps are over 201 *F
  8. Remove from heat and wrap in butcher paper or aluminum foil and let rest in a cooler for a minimum of 1-2 hours
  9. Remove from wrap, shred with forks and enjoy!
